Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Home Equity Investment?
A home equity investment lets homeowners get cash for part of their home's future value without debt. With Home.LLC, you get money now, pay no interest or monthly fees, and can use the cash however you like for 10 years.
What's the difference between an HEI and a loan?
Hometap invests in homes but doesn't give loans. You won't pay Home.LLC monthly or interest. It's a way to get funds for needs or opportunities without the worry of more monthly bills.
What is the process for getting a Home.LLC Investment?
Getting a Home.LLC Investment is easy:
1. Inquiry: Sign up and submit your inquiry on Hometap's site.
2. Talk: If you're a match, you'll get an estimate and chat with a manager about it.
3. Apply: Upload documents and apply online in 20 minutes.
4. Appraisal: We might need to check your property's value.
5. Get Funds: Sign the final papers and receive your money!
How long do I have to settle a Home.LLC Investment?
A Home.LLC Investment lasts for 30 years. You can settle it by using savings, getting a loan, or selling your house.
